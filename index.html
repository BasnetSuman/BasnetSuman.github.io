Welcome

I am a Research Fellow at the Clean Energy Research Platform (CERP), King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), where I work on high-pressure combustion, CFD, and scientific machine learning.

Research Interests

My work focuses on turbulent combustion, high-fidelity numerical simulation, and the integration of data-driven modeling for predictive flow analysis. I am particularly interested in applying physics-informed machine learning to model reacting flows in propulsion and energy systems.

Short Bio

Before joining KAUST, I earned my MSc in Space and Astronautical Engineering from Sapienza University of Rome and a B.Tech in Aerospace Engineering from India. My long-term goal is to pursue an academic career in mechanical and aerospace engineering, advancing the understanding of complex reactive flow systems.
